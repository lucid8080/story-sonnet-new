# The Keepers of Turtleshell City

## Series Identity
A cozy, wonder-filled audio adventure series about the maintenance crew who keep a whole walking city safe on the back of a gigantic gentle turtle.

## Tone
- imaginative
- cozy
- kid-friendly
- practical-magic
- wonder-filled
- warm teamwork

## Core Cast
- **Tavi** — thoughtful field lead, notices patterns and feelings in the city before others do
- **Imani** — gifted pipe-and-pressure engineer, steady, precise, deeply caring
- **Jun** — systems mapper and route planner, observant, calm, delightfully exacting
- **Suri** — bridge, rigging, and windworks specialist, clever with balance and knots
- **Niko** — enthusiastic junior Keeper, funny, brave, always learning by doing
- **Mossback Morning** — the gigantic turtle whose shell carries Turtleshell City across the world

## Series Rules
- The city and the turtle are both treated as living systems deserving care and respect.
- Problems should feel meaningful, but never frightening in a harsh way.
- Maintenance is heroic work.
- Small practical tasks can unlock big wonder.
- The city should feel richly inhabited, inclusive, and lovingly detailed.
- Endings should resolve with warmth, relief, and a sense of shared belonging.

## Voice Consistency
- Use **EN - Jane, Curious** for the whole series unless Andre changes direction.
- Do not speak age groups in the audio.
- Do not speak episode numbers in the audio.
- Save a final narration script inside each episode folder.

## Visual Direction
- Broad shell-top city with lantern streets, rooftop gardens, bridges, pipes, wind-catchers, and terraced homes.
- Mossback Morning should feel ancient, gentle, immense, and safe.
- Represent residents with broad natural inclusiveness in skin tones, attire, and hair textures/styles.
- Keep the palette cozy: moss green, warm teal, brass, terracotta, apricot lantern light, twilight violet.

# Cover Art Prompt — The Keepers of Turtleshell City

Create a **4:5 vertical** children's-book cover illustration for an audio story series titled **"The Keepers of Turtleshell City"**.

## Scene
A gigantic gentle turtle is walking across a sunlit meadow at golden hour, carrying an entire cozy city on its mossy shell. On the shell are lantern streets, tiny bridges, rooftop gardens, copper pipes, water tanks, wind-catchers, terraced houses, and little maintenance walkways. In the foreground on the shell, the main maintenance crew stands together with tools, looking out with curiosity and care.

## Main Characters to Feature
- **Tavi** — dark curls in two puff-braids, saffron scarf, patch-pocket overalls
- **Imani** — long locs wrapped in a patterned blue band, copper engineering tools, warm steady presence
- **Jun** — straight black hair tucked behind one ear, moss-green jacket with brass toggles, observant posture
- **Suri** — long thick braid with red thread woven through it, practical climbing gear, wind-and-rigging specialist
- **Niko** — chestnut curls, cheerful expression, carrying a lantern wrench or toolkit

## Style
- modern whimsical children's-book illustration
- hand-painted textured digital art
- gouache / watercolor / colored-pencil feel
- loose imperfect linework
- cozy muted-but-rich palette
- visible grain and brush texture
- decorative, poster-like composition
- emotionally warm and wonder-filled

## Composition Notes
- The turtle must feel enormous, ancient, kind, and safe.
- The city should feel lived-in and lovingly maintained, not crowded or grim.
- Show a sense of movement and scale: the world below, the city above, the crew as small but important caretakers.
- Keep character representation broad and natural.
- Leave room for title text at the upper portion of the cover.

## Palette Suggestions
Moss green, teal, brass, terracotta, apricot lantern light, cream, dusk violet.

## Output
- 4:5 aspect ratio
- cover-ready composition
- no scary elements
- no text baked into the image unless specifically requested

If final generation is unavailable, keep this prompt as the source-of-truth for later cover production.

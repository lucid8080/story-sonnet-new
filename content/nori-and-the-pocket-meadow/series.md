# Nori and the Pocket Meadow

## Series Identity
A cozy imaginative adventure series about Nori, a gentle child caretaker of a fold-out pocket meadow where tiny creatures, drifting seeds, and shy weather feelings come for help when the world gets a little out of tune.

## Recurring World Logic
- Nori carries a moss-green satchel that unfolds into a hidden meadow at twilight.
- Small creatures and meadow magic ask for help with emotional, sensory, and seasonal problems.
- Problems are solved through listening, patience, and playful practical magic.
- Endings should feel soft, satisfied, and ready for another visit to the meadow.

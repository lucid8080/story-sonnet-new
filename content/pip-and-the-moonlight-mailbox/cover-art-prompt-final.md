# Pip and the Moonlight Mailbox — Final Nano Banana Prompt Pack

## Primary Prompt
Create a **4:5 vertical children's book cover illustration** for a cozy bedtime audio story called **Pip and the Moonlight Mailbox**.

The image should use a **modern whimsical children’s book style** with a strong handcrafted storybook feel. Make it look like a **hand-painted textured digital illustration** that blends **gouache, watercolor, and colored pencil**. The mood should feel **soft, playful, emotionally warm, magical, and safe for young children**.

### Scene
Show a **moonlit garden path** outside a **little yellow house** at night.
At the center of the scene is a **tiny magical blue mailbox** glowing softly from within, with **warm golden light** spilling out from its small silver door.

Standing near the mailbox is **Pip**, a child in **moon-and-star pajamas** and **red rain boots**, holding a glowing letter and looking delighted, curious, and quietly brave.

Include these whimsical details naturally in the scene:
- sleepy daisies along the garden path
- mint leaves and soft garden greenery
- a few glowing fireflies
- a hedgehog peeking from blackberry bushes
- a small friendly cloud overhead with faint silvery rain sparkle

### Art Direction
- loose, imperfect organic linework
- rounded, simplified anatomy
- gentle asymmetry for personality and charm
- expressive but minimal facial features
- rosy cheeks, tiny nose, simple warm smile
- decorative, poster-like composition
- layered painterly brushwork with visible grain and texture
- subtle hand-drawn botanical patterns in the background
- clean focal point that reads clearly at thumbnail size

### Color Palette
Use a cozy muted-but-rich palette:
- moonlit blues
- soft teals
- warm cream
- burnt orange accents
- dusty purple
- gentle golden glow

Avoid bright neon colors or harsh primary-color saturation.

### Mood
The image should feel:
- cozy
- magical
- bedtime-friendly
- imaginative
- full of gentle wonder
- emotionally warm

### Composition
- 4:5 vertical format
- polished storybook cover composition
- flowers, leaves, and garden shapes can frame Pip and the mailbox
- curves and plant shapes should guide the eye inward
- leave some calm breathing room for title placement later
- keep the focal area centered and readable

## Negative Prompt / Avoid
Do not include:
- photorealism
- scary expressions
- horror atmosphere
- harsh shadows
- cluttered composition
- glossy 3D rendering
- anime style
- comic-book style
- realistic adult anatomy
- text baked into the image
- watermark
- overly dark nighttime scene

## Suggested Output Notes
- Format: PNG
- Aspect ratio: 4:5
- Intended use: story tile + story detail page cover
- Audience: children ages 4–6

# Nano Banana Cover Art Prompt

Create a **4:5 vertical children's book cover illustration** in a **modern whimsical storybook style** with a handcrafted, painterly feel.

## Core Style Direction
- hand-painted textured digital illustration
- feels like a blend of gouache, watercolor, and colored pencil
- soft, playful, emotionally warm, and clearly made for children's storytelling
- modern indie picture-book energy
- subtle Scandinavian / folk illustration influence
- inspired by the emotional warmth of artists like Oliver Jeffers or Rebecca Green, while remaining original

## Line Work & Shape Language
- loose, imperfect, organic linework
- nothing too rigid, sharp, or overly polished
- rounded, simplified anatomy
- gentle asymmetry for charm and personality
- expressive silhouette that reads well even at thumbnail size

## Color Palette
Use a cozy, muted-but-rich palette:
- moonlit blues
- soft teals
- warm cream
- burnt orange accents
- dusty purple
- gentle golden glow

Avoid loud neon colors or harsh bright primaries.

## Texture & Brushwork
- visible paper grain and brush texture throughout
- layered painterly strokes
- subtle colored-pencil detail in small areas
- hand-drawn botanical textures in the background
- everything should feel tactile and lovingly illustrated, not glossy or plastic

## Character Design
Pip should feel expressive, simple, and adorable:
- child character with rounded proportions
- expressive but minimal facial features
- rosy cheeks
- tiny nose
- simple warm smile
- emotion-first, not realism-first

## Scene
A moonlit garden path outside a little yellow house at night.
In the foreground is a tiny magical blue mailbox glowing softly from within, with golden light spilling out through its little silver door.
Pip stands nearby in moon-and-star pajamas and red rain boots, holding a glowing letter and looking delighted and curious.
Include whimsical nighttime details worked naturally into the composition:
- sleepy daisies
- mint leaves
- soft glowing fireflies
- a hedgehog peeking from blackberry bushes
- a small friendly cloud overhead with faint silvery rain sparkle

## Composition
- 4:5 vertical composition
- polished storybook cover layout
- flat decorative composition with a poster-like clarity
- flowers, leaves, and garden shapes can frame the character and mailbox
- curves and plant shapes should guide the eye inward
- keep the focal point clear at small thumbnail size
- leave some calm breathing room for title placement if needed later

## Mood
- cozy
- magical
- safe
- bedtime-friendly
- full of gentle wonder

## Avoid
- photorealism
- scary expressions
- horror vibes
- cluttered scene design
- overly dark shadows
- hard glossy 3D rendering
- text baked into image
- watermark

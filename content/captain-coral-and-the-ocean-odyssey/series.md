# Captain Coral and the Ocean Odyssey

## Series Identity
A fun, adventurous, and educational underwater series about Captain Coral, a brave and curious sea turtle who explores the ocean with her crew of fish friends. Each episode features a catchy repeating phrase that kids can shout along, gentle problem-solving, and ocean conservation themes.

## Tone
- fun
- adventurous
- educational
- rhythmic
- interactive
- optimistic

## Core Cast
- **Captain Coral** — a wise and adventurous green sea turtle with a shell patterned like a map of the oceans
- **Finley** — a hyperactive yellow tang fish who loves to race and tell jokes
- **Shelly** — a thoughtful hermit crab who carries a tiny library of ocean facts in her shell
- **Blink** — a shy bioluminescent jellyfish who lights up when excited or scared

## Series Rules
- Keep the tone upbeat, rhythmic, and participatory — kids should want to shout the repeating phrase.
- Each episode includes a simple ocean fact or conservation idea (e.g. "plastics hurt turtles", "coral reefs are homes").
- Adventures should be exciting but never scary — problems are solved through teamwork, curiosity, and kindness.
- The repeating phrase should be easy to remember, fun to say, and tied to the episode's theme.
- Narration should be lively and expressive, with room for sound effects and pauses for audience participation.

## Voice Consistency
- Use **EN - Bella, Adventure** (or similar lively, expressive voice) for Captain Coral’s narration unless direction changes.
- Do not speak age groups in the audio.
- Do not speak episode numbers in the audio.
- Save a final narration script inside each episode folder.

## Visual Direction
- Bright, colorful, and expressive underwater animation style
- Palette: ocean blues, coral pinks, sunny yellows, seaweed greens
- Lighting: dappled sunlight from above, bioluminescent glows in dark zones
- Key visual: Captain Coral’s ship — a giant hollowed-out log upgraded with sail, rudder, and a periscope made from a seashell.

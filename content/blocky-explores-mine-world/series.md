# Blocky Explores Mine World

## Series Identity
A playful audio adventure series set in a bright block-built world full of crafting, exploring, funny mishaps, strange creatures, hidden places, and teamwork.

## Tone
- adventurous
- playful
- clever
- slightly chaotic
- energetic but still kid-safe

## Core Cast
- **Blocky** — curious builder-brain lead, enthusiastic, bold, sometimes overconfident
- **Peb** — practical friend, notices weak plans and structural problems early
- **Glow** — loves shiny objects, rare finds, and discovery
- **Zip** — quick, funny, impulsive, often turns small moments into bigger ones
- **Moss** — calm, nature-loving, observant, steadying presence

## Series Rules
- More active and energetic than Pip
- Strong group dynamic
- Problem-solving and discovery are key
- Danger should feel exciting or goofy, not genuinely scary
- Endings should still land with emotional clarity and warmth


## Voice Consistency
- Episode 1 used **EN - Jane, Curious**
- Episode 2 uses **EN - Paul, Neutral**
- Unless Andre changes direction, use **EN - Paul, Neutral** for future Blocky episodes so the series settles into a consistent narrator voice.
- Do not speak age groups or episode numbers in Blocky audio.
- Always save the final narration script inside each episode folder.

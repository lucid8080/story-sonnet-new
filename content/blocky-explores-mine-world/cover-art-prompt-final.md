# Blocky Explores Mine World — Episode 1 Final Cover Prompt

Create a **4:5 vertical children's book cover illustration** for a playful audio adventure series called **Blocky Explores Mine World**.

The image should feel like a **modern whimsical children's-book illustration** with a handcrafted, storybook feel, but adapted to a **blocky voxel-inspired world**. It should still feel warm, painterly, and charming rather than hard-edged or game-rendered.

## Scene
Show **Blocky** and friends building a fort in a bright block-built outdoor world.
The fort should clearly be made from **tiny 1 by 1 blocks**, with a playful handmade look rather than realistic architecture.
Include a half-finished but charming little fort with ladders, blocks, a small flag, and funny details that suggest this build got a little ambitious.

Include some of Blocky’s recurring friends nearby:
- Peb, practical and steady
- Glow, excited by shiny blocks
- Zip, energetic and impulsive
- Moss, gentle and calm

The world should feel like a colorful block-based crafting/exploration world inspired by voxel sandbox adventures, but not like direct game box art.

## Style
- hand-painted textured digital illustration
- whimsical children's book look
- gouache / watercolor / colored-pencil feel
- soft but rich color palette
- loose organic linework layered over simplified blocky forms
- expressive faces and warm emotional energy
- decorative but readable composition
- clear focal point at thumbnail size

## Mood
- adventurous
- playful
- bright
- collaborative
- imaginative
- kid-friendly

## Color palette
Use bright but tasteful adventure colors:
- grassy greens
- sky blues
- warm golds
- earthy browns
- pops of glowing treasure color

## Avoid
- photorealism
- direct Minecraft branding or logos
- horror vibes
- harsh realism
- cluttered unreadable composition
- text baked into image
- watermark

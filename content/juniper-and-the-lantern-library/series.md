# Juniper and the Lantern Library

## Series Identity
A cozy magical library-cart series set around Clover Market, where Juniper helps the Lantern Library solve unusual story problems with listening, care, and a little evening magic.

## Tone
- cozy
- bookish
- gently magical
- emotionally clear
- calm but curious

## Core Cast
- **Juniper** — observant, kind, a little hesitant at first, but brave when something needs gentle care
- **Miss Marabel** — wise librarian with steady warmth, curious pockets, and a talent for calm solutions
- **Catalog** — ginger library cat, stern-eyed supervisor, quiet helper when it counts
- **Clover Market townsfolk** — soft supporting cast whose small sounds and habits help shape each story

## Series Rules
- Adventures begin around the Lantern Library at dusk.
- Problems should feel unusual but gentle: a shelf out of sorts, a story gone wandering, a bookmark missing its book, a lantern forgetting its glow.
- Solutions should come through listening, rhythm, patience, kindness, and noticing the right small detail.
- The world should feel handcrafted, warm, and full of little sensory details.
- Endings should land in a calm, lantern-lit image.

## Voice Consistency
- Use **EN - Paul, Neutral** for this series unless Andre changes direction.
- Do not speak age groups or episode numbers in Juniper audio.
- Always save the final narration script in the episode folder.

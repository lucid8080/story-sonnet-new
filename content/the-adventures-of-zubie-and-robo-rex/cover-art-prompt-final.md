# Cover Art Prompt — The Adventures of Zubie and Robo-Rex

Create a **4:5 vertical** children’s-book cover illustration for an audio story series titled **"The Adventures of Zubie and Robo-Rex"**.

## Scene
A cozy bedroom at bedtime glowing with warm lamplight. Zubie sits on a soft rug or bed in rocket-pattern pajamas, smiling beside Robo-Rex, his small blue robot dinosaur toy with green glowing eyes. Nearby, Dad is close by in the doorway or kneeling beside them with a warm, caring smile, creating a feeling of safety and love. The room should feel playful and lived-in, with books, a blanket, toy blocks, and a gentle nighttime atmosphere.

## Main Characters to Feature
- **Zubie** — 6-year-old Black boy, warm brown skin, short curly hair, expressive eyes, wearing cozy rocket pajamas
- **Dad** — Black man in his early 30s, dark brown skin, short beard, warm eyes, casual home clothes, gentle smile
- **Robo-Rex** — small blue robot dinosaur toy with rounded friendly design and glowing green eyes

## Style
- whimsical, painterly children’s illustration
- soft edges
- expressive faces
- warm, emotionally satisfying composition
- hand-painted texture feel
- gentle storybook charm

## Palette and Lighting
- golden yellows
- sky blues
- gentle browns
- warm lamplight indoors
- cozy bedtime glow

## Composition Notes
- Make the friendship between Zubie and Robo-Rex immediately clear.
- Dad should feel loving and supportive, not distant.
- Preserve the family’s Black identity naturally and clearly.
- Leave room for title text near the upper portion.
- The image should feel like the beginning of a comforting adventure series.
- Include this consistency anchor in the visual interpretation: “Consistent characters throughout the book — Zubie (6-year-old Black boy with curly hair and rocket pajamas), Dad (Black man, short beard, warm smile), Robo-Rex (small blue robot dinosaur with green eyes).”

## Output
- 4:5 aspect ratio
- cover-ready composition
- no scary elements
- no text baked into the image unless specifically requested

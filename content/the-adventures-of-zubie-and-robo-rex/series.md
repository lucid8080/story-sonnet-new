# The Adventures of Zubie and Robo-Rex

## Series Identity
A cozy, playful audio adventure series about Zubie and his little blue robot dinosaur best friend, where everyday problems become warm mysteries, tiny adventures, and loving discoveries.

## Tone
- cozy
- playful
- gentle
- emotionally warm
- curious
- bedtime-friendly

## Core Cast
- **Zubie** — a curious 6-year-old Black boy with warm brown skin, short curly hair, expressive eyes, and a big imagination
- **Dad** — Zubie’s caring, handy father with dark brown skin, a short beard, warm eyes, and a calm steady presence
- **Robo-Rex** — a small blue robot dinosaur toy with green glowing eyes, rounded friendly design, and cheerful beeps

## Series Rules
- Keep Zubie and his family grounded, loving, and emotionally real.
- Everyday home moments can feel magical without denying the practical love behind them.
- Problems should feel meaningful to a child, but never harsh or scary.
- Curiosity, observation, and kindness should solve more than force.
- Endings should land with warmth, relief, and a sense of being cared for.

## Voice Consistency
- Use **EN - Jane, Curious** for the whole series unless direction changes.
- Do not speak age groups in the audio.
- Do not speak episode numbers in the audio.
- Save a final narration script inside each episode folder.

## Visual Direction
- Whimsical, painterly children’s illustration with soft edges and expressive faces
- Palette: golden yellows, sky blues, gentle browns
- Lighting: warm lamplight indoors, soft sunrise glow in morning scenes
- Character consistency phrase: “Consistent characters throughout the book — Zubie (6-year-old Black boy with curly hair and rocket pajamas), Dad (Black man, short beard, warm smile), Robo-Rex (small blue robot dinosaur with green eyes).”
